About Me

I’m Md. Shariful Islam, a Sales Representative at Beige Corporation, currently pursuing a BSc in Information Technology and Management at Daffodil International University. My background combines a passion for technology with practical experience in sales, sustainability, and leadership. I co-founded ODOMMO '19, an initiative focused on social impact, and have worked with organizations like the International Youth Development Society and the International Youth Math Challenge.

I’ve had the opportunity to study abroad in Sweden, the USA, Russia, and Malaysia, which has broadened my perspective. This multicultural experience helps me connect with people from various backgrounds and contribute to global conversations on sustainability and youth empowerment.

Outside of my professional life, I’m deeply interested in social impact, innovation, and education. I believe in the power of collaboration and education to address global challenges, and I’m dedicated to creating positive change through community-driven initiatives and continuous learning.
